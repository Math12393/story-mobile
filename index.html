A mobile-optimized interactive story. Help Agent complete the mission. The final message is classified.

The Briefing

"Agent, your mission is to retrieve the encrypted drive from the old safe house. Intelligence suggests it contains vital information. You must move quickly and quietly."

Swipe or use the buttons to navigate.

Stealth Approach

Under the cover of twilight, you approach the safe house. The neighborhood is silent. You use the rooftops, avoiding the streetlights and security cameras below.

Inside the Safe House

You pick the lock on the back entrance. Inside, the air is stale and dusty. A single desk sits in the middle of the room, and against the far wall is the old safe mentioned in the brief.

Cracking the Safe

The combination is more complex than expected. After several tense minutes, you hear a satisfying *click*. The heavy door swings open, revealing a single, sleek data drive inside.

The Decrypted Message

You plug the drive into your portable decoder. "Decryption complete. Primary file is a high-priority visual transmission. Playing now..." The screen flickers as the file loads.
